StretchIt

Do some stretches to help relieve stress and prevent stress-related injuries

Step by Step Process

  1. Put your hands in prayer position.
  2. Spread fingers apart as far as you can, but keep fingers together.
  3. Repeat ten times.
  1. Shake hands like you've just cleaned them.
  2. Repeat for a minute or two.
  1. Place one arm straight out, wrist extended and fingers facing down.
  2. Spread your fingers slightly and use your other hand to apply pressure down, stretching your wrist and fingers as far as you're able.
  3. When you reach your maximum point of flexibility, hold this position for 20 seconds.
  4. Switch hands and repeat five times.
